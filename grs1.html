GR-S1

Granular Synthesizer

GR-S1

GR-S1 is a powerful granular synthesizer packed into the small and familiar form of a cassette tape. This is a project that I am currently working on and is not yet complete. The instrument works by taking in live audio, granulizing it, and then playing those rearranged grains via midi controller. The synth was prototyped in Max MSP, before being ported to Pure Data, in order to be run on a Raspberry Pi Zero. Kicad was used to design a PCB that could accommodate the Raspberry Pi Zero, physical controls, various DACs/ADCs, and components. The final product will be housed in an aluminum case. Using CAD, a prototype case has been developed and 3D printed. This website will be updated as the prject progresses.
