Keanu Yokoyama

Audio engineer, producer, sound designer, musician, and artist; Keanu Yokoyama, creates art from sound. Keanu Originates from the northern coast of Oregon, and is currently exploring the art scene in Portland. Keanu has earned his degree in Music and Sonic Arts, and certificate of Creative Coding & Immersive Technologies from Portland Community College. Currently, Keanu studies Sonic Arts and Music Production at Portland State University. Keanu enjoys capturing and designing unique sounds to bring his client's vision to life.

Keanu’s work can be seen in experiential art installments, instrument design, and sound design. To facilitate his work, Keanu utilizes his experience with creative coding and knowledge of programs such as, Pure Data, Max MSP, Arduino, Processing, and Computer-aided design software, electronics, and microcontrollers.

In the studio, Keanu’s experience includes recording, engineering, mixing, and producing. Keanu works collaboratively with artists to create original music, concepts, and sounds. Keanu's audio engineering reaches into the world of sound for picture with projects in field recording, post production, ADR, and Foley.
