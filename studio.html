About the Studio

Nestled in the small Pacific Northwest town of Astoria, Oregon, is Foxglove recording studio. This small project studio is equipped with everything needed to create and record. Merging the past with the present, Foxglove incorporates vintage gear, such as their Tascam M520 mixing console and Tascam 38 8-Track tape machine, with the modern world of DAWs and digital processing. This combination allows for vintage sound, with the conveniences of modern editing. Foxglove is also equipped with an assortment of microphones, and other instruments.

While the studio is small, the space is more than capable for the singer-songwriter, small band, or any one looking to record audio and complete their project. This space creates an intimate experience and welcoming athmosphere.

Keanu Yokoyama, also known under the monicker Kokonama, is the lead engineer at Foxglove. Keanu's endeavors include music production, audio engineering, musical performance and sound design. Read more about Keanu in his portfolio.
