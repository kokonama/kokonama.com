Wildcats

Wildcats are a group of friends who started a club where they would rap during their lunch period. As a member Portland State University's "Advocating for the Arts" capstone class, myself and a team of consisting of Leah Maldanado, Jack Stringer, and Jennifer Wyant spend one lunch period a week with these kids, in order to work on their music skills. We provided workshops, and practice sessions with the goal that by the end of the term, the kids would be able to record a few songs.

We recorded 2 songs at Portland Community College's recording studio, where I acted as audio engineer, and mix engineer. The beats were made by community memebrs, and were donated for the Wildcats to use.
